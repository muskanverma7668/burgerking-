About Us

Welcome to BurgerKing, your go-to spot for mouth-watering burgers, crispy fries, and refreshing beverages in Haridwar! Located near SMJN PG College, we bring you the authentic taste of classic and contemporary burgers, all made with the freshest ingredients and a pinch of love.

Founded by Muskan Verma, BurgerKing is dedicated to serving not just food, but memorable experiences. Whether you’re a student, a family, or a food lover exploring the city, we promise you a delightful culinary journey every time you visit.
